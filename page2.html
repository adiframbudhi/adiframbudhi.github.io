Intelligences Test

  • CFIT
  • IST

The Culture Fair Intelligence Test (CFIT) was created by Raymond Cattell in 1949 as an attempt to measure cognitive abilities devoid of sociocultural and environmental influences comprises both fluid intelligence (Gf) and crystallized intelligence (Gc). Highly recommended for general employee selection.

The intelligence structure test (IST) has been developed in 1953 By Rudolf Amthauer for tasks of occupational selection and vocational guidance. The test allows to receive complete representation about individual´s intelligence. the test allow us to predict success of educational activity; to help with a choice of a trade, a choice of a level of training; to predict success of professional occupation, including trades demanding special intellectual skills. Highly recommended for vocational and technical recruit.

Personality Test

  • MBTI
  • PAPI-Kostick
  • Big-five
  • EPPS

The purpose of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator® (MBTI®) personality inventory is to make the theory of psychological types described by C. G. Jung understandable and useful in people's lives. The essence of the theory is that much seemingly random variation in the behavior is actually quite orderly and consistent, being due to basic differences in the ways individuals prefer to use their perception and judgment. Highly recommended for management recruit

Personality test which is used to determine a person's personality traits in the workplace. The questionnaire was designed to reveal a person's behavior's and preferences that may affect their suitability for a various vocations or specific vacant positions. Highly recommended for general employee selection.

Mobirise is an easy and simple free website builder - just drop site elements to your page, add content and style it to look the way you like. Highly recommended for Front office, Customer Services employee selection.

Edwards Personal Preference Schedule (EPPS) is a forced choice, objective, non-projective personality inventory. measures the importance of a need that is personal and relative to the individual. Highly recommended general recruit.

Work Preference Test

  • Job Knowledge
  • Working Culture & Value
  • Working Interest & Motivation

Job knowledge test is an additional test that can be done to select employees with certain types of work such as programmer, marketing, graphic designer. can be developed according to company needs.

The work value test is a test that is carried out if the company requires employees who are in line with the company's values. highly recommended to create a good corporate culture.

Selection of job interests is highly recommended to be developed within the company. very suitable for employee orientation and finding out training needs.

Projective Test

  • Draw A Tree Test
  • HTP Test
  • Wartegg Test

Projective test with commands to draw trees. This test will show whether the personality of the prospective employee can be projected properly. Needed to ensure that the personality of the prospective employee has parallels with the results of other tests

Wartegg Drawing Completion Test (WDCT) is one of the most commonly used personality analysis tools of graphology, which is the mapping from the inside world to personal qualities. It helps institutes or individuals to have a better knowledge of intrinsic personality characters.

Performance Test

  • Kreapelin
  • Accuracy test

Kreapelin is an arithmetic-based test developed by Emile Kraepelin with the aim of finding the performance of workers. highly recommended for jobs recruit that require high precision, high work resistance, and administration.

Similar to the kreapelin test, the accuracy test is carried out to find out how accurate the prospective employee is in comparing two statements. Highly recommended for administration, warehouse, and pharmacy recruitment.
